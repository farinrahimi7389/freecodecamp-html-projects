How to Make Homemade Rice Krispies

Ever wondered what to bring to a potluck that's easy to make and delicious? Then look no further because we've got the perfect recipe for you!

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of mini marshmallows
  • 5 cups of crispy rice cereal
  • 1/4 cup of butter
  • Melted chocolate
  • Sprinkles!!

Instructions

  1. Melt butter in a large saucepan over low heat.
  2. Add marshmallows and stir until melted and well combined. Cook 2 minutes longer, stirring constantly. Remove from heat.
  3. Stir in crispy rice cereal until well coated.
  4. Press mixture evenly and firmly into a buttered 9x13-inch pan using a buttered spatula or waxed paper.
  5. Melt chocolate using the microwave.
  6. Decorate the surface with sprinkles and melted chocolate to give it a fun design!
  7. Cut into 2-inch squares when cool.
